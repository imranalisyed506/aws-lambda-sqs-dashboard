# Frequently Asked Questions

## General Questions

### What is CloudStore?

CloudStore is a modern e-commerce platform that provides a complete solution for online retail. We offer product management, order processing, secure payments, and customer management all in one platform.

### How much does it cost?

We offer three pricing tiers:

- **Starter**: $29/month - Up to 100 products, 1000 orders/month
- **Professional**: $99/month - Unlimited products, 10,000 orders/month
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing - Unlimited everything, dedicated support

All plans include a 14-day free trial with no credit card required.

### Is there a free trial?

Yes! All plans come with a 14-day free trial. You can test all features with no commitment. No credit card is required to start your trial.

### What payment methods do you accept?

We accept:
- Credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
- Debit cards
- PayPal
- Bank transfers (Enterprise plans)

### Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. There are no cancellation fees. Your service will continue until the end of your billing period.

## Technical Questions

### What integrations do you support?

We integrate with:
- **Payment**: Stripe, PayPal, Square
- **Shipping**: FedEx, UPS, USPS, DHL
- **Marketing**: Mailchimp, SendGrid, Google Analytics
- **Accounting**: QuickBooks, Xero
- **CRM**: Salesforce, HubSpot

### Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use:
- 256-bit SSL encryption for all data transmission
- Encrypted database storage
- Regular security audits
- SOC 2 Type II certification
- PCI DSS compliance for payment data
- Daily automated backups

### What's your uptime guarantee?

We guarantee 99.9% uptime for all Professional and Enterprise plans. If we don't meet this guarantee, you'll receive service credits.

Our average uptime over the past 12 months: 99.97%

### Do you offer an API?

Yes! We provide a comprehensive REST API with detailed documentation. The API is available on all plans and includes:
- Product management
- Order management
- Customer management
- Webhook notifications
- Rate limiting based on your plan

### Can I import my existing products?

Yes, we support bulk import via:
- CSV files
- Excel spreadsheets
- API import
- Direct migration from other platforms

We also offer free migration assistance for Enterprise customers.

### What happens if I exceed my plan limits?

You'll receive an email notification when you reach 80% of your limits. If you exceed your limits:
- **Products**: You can upgrade or remove products
- **Orders**: Overage charged at $0.10 per order
- **API calls**: Rate limiting applies, upgrade for higher limits

## Account Questions

### How do I upgrade my plan?

1. Log into your account
2. Go to Settings → Billing
3. Click "Upgrade Plan"
4. Select your new plan
5. Confirm

Changes take effect immediately, and you'll only pay the prorated difference.

### Can I downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can downgrade at any time. The change will take effect at the end of your current billing cycle. Make sure your usage fits within the lower plan's limits before downgrading.

### What if I forgot my password?

1. Go to the login page
2. Click "Forgot Password"
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your email for reset link
5. Create a new password

### How do I delete my account?

We're sorry to see you go! To delete your account:
1. Go to Settings → Account
2. Scroll to "Danger Zone"
3. Click "Delete Account"
4. Confirm deletion

Note: This action is permanent and cannot be undone. All data will be deleted within 30 days.

## Shipping Questions

### What shipping carriers do you support?

We integrate with:
- FedEx (all services)
- UPS (all services)
- USPS (Priority, Express, First Class)
- DHL (International)
- Custom carriers (Enterprise plans)

### Can I offer free shipping?

Yes! You can configure shipping rules based on:
- Order total (e.g., free over $50)
- Customer location
- Product categories
- Promotional codes

### How do tracking numbers work?

Tracking numbers are:
- Automatically generated when you create a shipment
- Sent to customers via email
- Visible in their order history
- Updated in real-time

### Do you support international shipping?

Yes, we support shipping to over 200 countries. Features include:
- Automatic customs documentation
- International carrier integration
- Currency conversion
- VAT/duty calculation
