# Getting Started with CloudStore

Welcome to CloudStore! This guide will help you set up your store and make your first sale in under an hour.

## Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Visit [cloudstore.com/signup](https://cloudstore.com/signup)
2. Enter your email and create a password
3. Choose your store name (you can change this later)
4. Select your country and currency
5. Click "Create Store"

**Pro tip**: Use a business email if possible. This builds customer trust.

---

## Step 2: Store Setup

### Basic Information

1. Go to **Settings → Store Details**
2. Fill in:
   - Store name
   - Store description (appears in search results)
   - Contact email
   - Business address
   - Phone number

### Logo and Branding

1. Go to **Settings → Branding**
2. Upload your logo (recommended: 400x400px PNG)
3. Choose brand colors (primary and accent)
4. Select fonts for your store

**Don't have a logo?** Use our free logo generator in the dashboard!

---

## Step 3: Add Your First Product

1. Click **Products → Add Product**
2. Fill in product details:

**Required fields**:
- Product name
- Price
- SKU (Stock Keeping Unit)
- At least one image

**Recommended fields**:
- Description (use formatting to highlight features)
- Category
- Tags (helps customers find products)
- Weight (for shipping calculation)
- Inventory quantity

### Product Images

Upload 3-7 high-quality images:
- First image is the main product photo
- Include different angles
- Show product in use if possible
- Use white or neutral background
- Minimum 1000x1000px recommended

### Pricing Strategy

Consider:
- Product cost
- Shipping costs
- Your desired profit margin
- Competitor pricing

**Example calculation**:
```
Product cost: $20
Shipping: $5
Platform fees (3%): $0.75
Desired profit: $10
---
Selling price: $36
```

---

## Step 4: Configure Shipping

1. Go to **Settings → Shipping**
2. Choose shipping strategy:

### Option A: Flat Rate
Simple and predictable. Charge same rate for all orders.

**Best for**: Small, similar-weight products

**Setup**:
- Click "Add Shipping Zone"
- Select countries
- Set flat rate (e.g., $5)

### Option B: Calculated Rates
Show real-time rates from carriers.

**Best for**: Varied product sizes/weights

**Setup**:
- Connect carrier account (FedEx, UPS, etc.)
- Enable calculated rates
- Set handling fee (optional)

### Option C: Free Shipping
Absorb shipping costs.

**Best for**: High-margin products, promotional periods

**Setup**:
- Create shipping rule
- Set minimum order (e.g., free over $50)

### Packaging

Add package dimensions for accurate rate calculation:
1. Go to **Settings → Shipping → Packages**
2. Add your standard box sizes
3. Set default package for new products

---

## Step 5: Set Up Payments

Accept payments by connecting a payment processor:

### Stripe (Recommended)

1. Go to **Settings → Payments**
2. Click **Connect Stripe**
3. Follow Stripe signup process
4. Return to CloudStore (automatic)

**Fees**: 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction

### PayPal

1. Go to **Settings → Payments**
2. Click **Connect PayPal**
3. Log into PayPal business account
4. Authorize connection

**Fees**: 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction

### Multiple Payment Methods

Enable several options to increase conversion:
- Credit/Debit cards (via Stripe)
- PayPal
- Apple Pay
- Google Pay

---

## Step 6: Customize Your Storefront

### Choose a Theme

1. Go to **Store Design → Themes**
2. Browse available themes
3. Click "Preview" to test
4. Click "Apply" to activate

**Free themes**: 12 professional designs included
**Premium themes**: $49-$149 one-time purchase

### Customize Design

1. Click **Customize** on active theme
2. Customize:
   - Header and navigation
   - Homepage layout
   - Product page design
   - Footer content
   - Colors and fonts

### Add Pages

Create essential pages:

**About Page**: Settings → Pages → Add Page
```markdown
# About [Your Store]

Tell your story:
- Who you are
- Why you started
- What makes you different
- Your values
```

**Shipping Policy**: Settings → Pages → Add Page
- Processing time
- Shipping methods
- International shipping
- Tracking information

**Return Policy**: Settings → Pages → Add Page
- Return window (e.g., 30 days)
- Condition requirements
- Refund process
- Exceptions

**Privacy Policy**: Use our template in Settings → Legal

---

## Step 7: Configure Notifications

Set up automated customer emails:

1. Go to **Settings → Notifications**
2. Customize email templates:
   - Order confirmation
   - Shipping notification
   - Delivery confirmation
   - Order cancellation
   - Refund processed

### Email Best Practices

- Use friendly, conversational tone
- Include clear order details
- Add tracking links
- Provide support contact
- Include your branding

---

## Step 8: Test Your Store

Before going live, test the complete purchase flow:

1. Click **View Store** to see customer view
2. Add product to cart
3. Proceed to checkout
4. Use test payment card:
   ```
   Card number: 4242 4242 4242 4242
   Expiry: Any future date
   CVC: Any 3 digits
   ```
5. Complete purchase
6. Check that you received order notification
7. Verify order appears in dashboard

**Important**: Enable test mode in Settings → Payments first!

---

## Step 9: Go Live!

Ready to accept real orders?

1. Go to **Settings → Payments**
2. Disable "Test Mode"
3. Go to **Settings → Store**
4. Toggle "Store Status" to **Live**
5. Remove any placeholder products

🎉 **Congratulations! Your store is live!**

---

## Step 10: Drive Traffic

Now that your store is live, attract customers:

### Immediate Actions

1. **Share on social media**: Post your store link
2. **Email friends and family**: Get your first customers
3. **Add to your email signature**: Promote everywhere
4. **Join relevant communities**: Reddit, Facebook groups

- TikTok: Product demos, tutorials

**SEO**:
- Write keyword-rich product descriptions
- Create blog content (Settings → Blog)
- Get listed on Google Shopping
- Build backlinks

**Paid Advertising**:
- Facebook Ads: Targeted audience
- Google Ads: Search intent
- Instagram Ads: Visual products
- Pinterest Ads: DIY and crafts

**Email Marketing**:
- Build email list (add signup form)
- Send weekly newsletters
- Abandoned cart emails (Professional plan)
- Promotional campaigns
